AirSight: AI-Powered Air Quality Prediction System

Overview: An intelligent web platform that predicts Air Quality Index (AQI) using machine learning, helping communities make informed decisions about outdoor activities and health precautions. Poor air quality contributes to respiratory diseases affecting millions worldwide.

Technical Implementation:

  • Dataset: EPA Air Quality System with 33+ environmental features
  • ML Algorithms: Implemented and compared 4 algorithms - Gradient Boosting, Random Forest, Extra Trees, and XGBoost
  • Model Performance: Achieved 96.2% accuracy (R² score) with the best-performing model
  • Pollutants Tracked: PM2.5, PM10, O₃ (Ozone), NO₂, SO₂, and CO
  • Tech Stack: Python, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, Flask/Django, JavaScript, Cloud Deployment

Key Features:

  • Real-time Predictions: Live AQI predictions with 7-day weather-integrated forecasts
  • Interactive Dashboards: Historical trend analysis with dynamic visualizations and calendar views
  • Pollutant Monitoring: Individual pollutant tracking and concentration analysis
  • Health Recommendations: Personalized suggestions based on current and predicted air quality levels
  • Model Insights: Professional dashboard displaying model performance metrics and comparison

Impact: Deployed as a production-ready cloud-based solution providing actionable environmental health insights for community well-being and proactive health measures.

Global Superstore – Revenue Optimization with Python

Revenue Optimization for Underperforming Regions

To enhance revenue in underperforming regions, a multi-faceted pricing strategy was developed using conditional business rules:

  • Demand-Based Adjustment: Prices were increased for high-demand items using a predefined uplift factor.
  • Premium Increase: Targeted price increments were applied to premium product categories to maximize margins.
  • Bundling Strategy: Discounts were offered on grouped products to encourage higher volume sales.
  • Dynamic Pricing: Prices were dynamically adjusted based on demand forecasts generated through ARIMA modeling.

Each strategy was programmatically mapped to relevant product segments using pre-defined tags. Adjusted sales values were then incorporated into forecasting models to evaluate revenue impact and effectiveness.
